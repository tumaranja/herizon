# Signup routing page

I saw [this LinkedIn post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/mariluukkainen_hire-diverse-pre-vetted-talent-share-7466407920717545474-Serd/): match.herizon.io is a signup form for companies (employers), but job seekers keep mistakenly making company profiles.

I mocked up a quick fix: a routing page in front of the form. Visitors pick "I'm hiring" or "I'm looking for work" and get sent to the right place before they ever see the form. It's styled to match the platform's existing branding and dark theme.

Check it out at [herizon-iota.vercel.app](https://herizon-iota.vercel.app)
